About

Computech A network consultant may be contracted to create a network from scratch or to take an existing network and enhance or update any or all components associated with the business tool. The consulting may involve nothing more than evaluating the needs of the client and preparing a detailed document of what is needed to create a networking structure that will meet those needs. In other instances, the network consultant may be contracted to actually see to the purchase of needed equipment, securing the right vendors for various communication services used within the network, and providing basic training to employees who will handle day to day troubleshooting and maintenance tasks.
